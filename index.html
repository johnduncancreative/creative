John Duncan

Graphic Designer at Simpleview

My experience is diverse from working for a non-profit, being a designer at a fast-paced sign shop to more recently working as a Graphic Designer at Simpleview, the industry leader of travel and tourism software.

I'm a graduate of Southwest University of Visual Arts with a BA in Graphic Design and an Advertising and Marketing minor. My greatest achievements are being a husband of 20 years and a father to four great children.
